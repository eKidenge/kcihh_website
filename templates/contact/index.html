Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our work.

How can I partner with KCIHH?

We welcome partnerships with organizations, companies, and institutions aligned with our mission. Please fill out the contact form or email us at partnerships@kcihh.org.

Do you accept volunteers?

Yes! We have various volunteer opportunities. Visit our Get Involved page or contact us directly to learn about current openings.

How do I apply for the incubation program?

Applications for our Climate Innovation Lab open twice a year. Check our Innovation Hub page for announcements and application deadlines.

Where do you operate in Kenya?

We work across 10 counties including arid, semi-arid, and agricultural regions. Our main office is in Nairobi with field offices in partner counties.
