Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our work.

How can I partner with KCIHH?

We welcome partnerships with organizations, companies, and institutions aligned with our mission. Please fill out the contact form or email us at partnerships@kcihh.org with details about your proposal.

Do you accept volunteers?

Yes! We have various volunteer opportunities in research, community engagement, and program support. Visit our Get Involved page or contact us directly to learn about current openings and application process.

How do I apply for the incubation program?

Applications for our Climate Innovation Lab open twice a year (March and September). Check our Innovation Hub page for announcements, eligibility criteria, and application deadlines. Selected startups receive mentorship, funding, and technical support.

Where do you operate in Kenya?

We work across 10 counties including arid (Turkana, Kajiado), semi-arid (Kitui, Makueni), and agricultural regions (Kiambu, Meru). Our main office is in Nairobi with field offices in partner counties and community-based program locations.

How can I support your work financially?

You can support us through one-time donations, monthly giving, or corporate partnerships. Visit our Donate page for secure online payments, or contact us for bank transfer details and sponsorship opportunities. All contributions are tax-deductible.
