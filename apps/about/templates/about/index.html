Who We Are

Bridging Innovation with
Indigenous Wisdom

Kenya Climate Innovation & Heritage Hub is a national youth-led institution dedicated to advancing climate resilience through innovation, heritage preservation, and community partnership.

We believe Kenya's climate future lies at the intersection of youth ingenuity and indigenous wisdom. Founded in 2020 by a coalition of young climate activists and community elders, we've grown into a nationally recognized platform for grassroots climate action.

Our Journey

The Path to Impact

From a bold idea to a national movement for climate action

2020 Founded

The Beginning

Started in Nairobi with 10 youth innovators and 5 community elders. Our first seed bank was established in Kitui County.

2022 Expansion

County Expansion

Launched regional hubs in 10 counties. First cohort of 20 climate startups graduated from our incubation program.

2023 Recognition

National Recognition

Recognized as Kenya's leading youth climate platform. Received UNEP accreditation and partnership with UNDP.

2024 Milestone

Impact Milestone

Reached 500+ youth trained, 50K trees planted, and 15 indigenous knowledge archives established.

2025 Present

Scaling Impact

Launching innovation labs in 5 new counties. Developing digital platform for indigenous knowledge documentation.
