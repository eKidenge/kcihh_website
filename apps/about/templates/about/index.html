Our Journey

From Vision to Impact

Born from a powerful realization: Kenya's climate future lies at the intersection of youth ingenuity and indigenous wisdom.

2020

Founded in Nairobi

Started with 10 youth innovators and 5 community elders sharing a vision for climate-resilient Kenya. Our first office was a small room in Nairobi, but our dreams were boundless.

First pilot project launched
2022

Expanded to 10 Counties

Launched regional hubs across Kenya, reaching communities in arid, semi-arid, and agricultural regions. Established partnerships with county governments.

500+ youth trained
2024

National Recognition

Recognized as Kenya's leading youth climate platform by UNDP. Our indigenous knowledge archive received international acclaim for preserving traditional wisdom.

15 knowledge archives created
