Our Story

Where Innovation Meets
Indigenous Wisdom

The Kenya Climate Innovation & Heritage Hub was born from a simple yet powerful realization: Kenya's climate future lies at the intersection of youth ingenuity and indigenous wisdom.

Founded in 2020 by a group of young climate activists and community elders, we've grown into a national platform that incubates climate innovations, preserves traditional ecological knowledge, and empowers grassroots communities.
